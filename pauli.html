Here is brief timeline of Acid Pauli's life

  • 1970s - Born in Germany/ Bavaria with name Martin Gretschmann
  • Late 1980s - Played bass in punk/indie band
  • 1995 - Released songs under the pseudonym "Console"
  • 1996 - Released first album "Pan or Ama" and joined band "The Notwist"
  • Late 90s/ Early 2000s - Started solo project "Acid Pauli", incorporated different kinds of music with house/techno
  • Around 2011 - Started a creative collaboration with "NU" and "The Feathered Sun".
